next ECA Student Leadership Program

Position/Title: Campus Ambassador

About the Program:

Are you passionate about creating opportunities, building communities, and making a difference on your campus?

The next ECA Student Leadership Program is designed for students who want to grow as leaders while helping others as well as themselves discover competitions, hackathons, Olympiads, scholarships, and other valuable extracurricular opportunities.

As a next ECA Campus Ambassador, you'll represent next ECA at your university, connect students with opportunities, build meaningful partnerships, and help create a stronger innovation ecosystem on your campus.

This isn't just another ambassador program, it's a chance to develop leadership, expand your network, and create real impact.

Whether you're passionate about leadership, community building, entrepreneurship, or innovation, this program gives you the opportunity to create real impact while growing personally and professionally.



Ready to make a difference? Here's what you'll do as a next ECA Campus Ambassador:



Your Responsibilities

Promote Opportunities:

Introduce next ECA to students on your campus.

Share valuable opportunities, updates, and initiatives through your university communities and social platforms.

Build Partnerships:

Connect next ECA with student clubs, organizations, and campus communities.

Help establish collaborations that benefit both students and partner organizations.



Lead Campus Initiatives:

Support or organize seminars, workshops, info sessions, hackathons, meetups, or other student activities.

Gain hands-on experience in leadership, communication, teamwork, and event management.

Spotlight Campus Success:

Discover inspiring student achievements, campus events, competitions, and success stories.

Share them with the next ECA team so we can celebrate and inspire thousands of students.

Be the Voice of Your Campus:

Collect feedback from students and clubs.

Help us continuously improve next ECA by sharing ideas and suggestions from your campus community.



Your Benefits

Being a next ECA Campus Ambassador means becoming part of a growing community of student leaders. You'll receive:

Official Certificate of Completion

LinkedIn Recommendation for outstanding contributors

Performance-based Recommendation Letter for higher studies and professional opportunities

Monthly Top Performer Recognition

Featured shout-outs across next ECA's social media platforms

Priority consideration for future Core Team positions, internships, and leadership roles

Valuable networking with students, clubs, founders, and professionals

Priority or discounted access to future workshops, seminars, newsletters, and community events

Personal guidance for hackathons, Olympiads, competitions, and extracurricular journeys

Exclusive goodies and merchandise for top performers

Future performance-based affiliate commissions and incentive programs as next ECA grows

Our Mission

Every year, countless students miss opportunities that could change their careers—not because they lack talent, but because they never knew those opportunities existed. At next ECA, we're changing that.

We're building a community where opportunities are easier to discover, knowledge is shared openly, and students help other students grow.

As a next ECA Campus Ambassador, you won't just represent a platform—you'll become a catalyst for your campus, connecting students with opportunities that can shape their future.

If you believe leadership is about creating opportunities for others, not just yourself, then we'd love to have you on this journey.

Together, let's make opportunities more visible, accessible, and community-driven.

next ECA — For the Community. By the Community.
